# Constellation Intelligence & Technologies (CINT)

**Constellation Intelligence & Technologies (CINT)** is a universe-inspired AI organization that builds constellation-like, multi-agent systems.  
We believe modern AI should not just be a single model, but an orchestrated set of specialized agents — like stars forming meaningful constellations.

Our work focuses on:
- a shared orchestration and state layer,
- reusable, domain-specific intelligent components,
- and developer-friendly tooling for building real AI systems.

CINT’s ecosystem is designed to let teams start from a solid multi-agent core and expand outward into different scenarios (customer service, document/policy intelligence, retrieval/RAG, enterprise workflows, etc.) without rebuilding the foundation every time.

---

## Mission

To create an extensible “constellation” of AI agents that can collaborate, observe, and evolve together, enabling organizations to build their own intelligent universe.

---

## What we build

- **Foundational frameworks** for multi-agent orchestration
- **Nebula / constellation-level solutions** for vertical scenarios
- **Planet-level connectors & tools** to integrate LLMs, vector DBs, and enterprise services
- **Observability and control** so AI behavior is traceable and operable

# 星座智能科技（Constellation Intelligence & Technologies，简称 CINT）

**CINT** 是一家以“星座式协同”为理念的 AI 技术组织，我们希望智能系统的形态更像一组有序排列的星体：每一个智能体都是独立且有价值的，同时又能在统一的编排之下协同工作，组成更大的“智能星系”。

我们重点做三件事：
1. 打造统一的多智能体编排与状态内核；
2. 沉淀可复用的场景化智能能力；
3. 提供对开发者友好的工具链，让 AI 真正能落在业务里。

通过这样的方式，企业或团队可以以一个通用的智能底座为起点，向外不断生长客服、文档治理、知识检索、企业流程等场景，而无需每次都从零搭一套 AI 系统。

---

## 我们的使命

构建一套可扩展、可协同、可观测的“智能星座”，让组织能够拥有自己的 AI 宇宙。

---

## 我们在做的

- 多智能体编排的基础框架
- 面向具体场景的“星云/星座级”解决方案
- 与各类大模型、向量库、企业服务对接的工具与适配器
- 让智能体运行轨迹可观测、可运维的控制台能力
